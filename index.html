Braemar Court is the illigitimate brain child of Shelley Gair, Morley MacDougall and Darren Reiter. It's a shoe string orphanage for delinquent film projects. We're in post production with our first film, 'The River Jordan', which is completely brilliant. Watch the trailer below and see for yourself.



Completed: The River Jordan - A dramatic short film, set in 1970. A young academic couple stop to pick up a pair of hitchhikers in the desert on their way back to campus. The film portrays the culture clash between the idealistic middle-class and the harsh reality of the working class with devastating results.

The River Jordan has been invited to screen at the Atlantic Film Festival. Atlantic Canadians are the bestest Canadians ever!

The River Jordan is on IMDB now. So, go and vote for it... often. We suggest a nine and a half because there are some cynical bastards out there who won't believe it actually is a ten.
